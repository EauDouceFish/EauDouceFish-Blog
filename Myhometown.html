My Hometown----Fuzhou

I live in fuzhou,which is a second-tier coastal city as the capital city of Fujian Province.There are many beautiful places and scenic spots to visit in Fuzhou, like Nanhoujie,Shangxiahang (Some ancient-style attractions),etc.
These scenic spots are good places for daily travel and relaxation. In my spare time, such as weekend evenings, I often take the subway to these scenic spots with my close friends.
Hanging out!(joking) Having a drink,eat some snacks and enjoy the cool night.Fantastic!

The two pictures on the right are Nanhoujie and Shangxiahang respectively.These two wonderful places are very popular with people. But I am more glad to enjoy it alone--early morning,evening,or rainy days.Less people would disturb my own world.It's the time for relaxation.

Of course,as they are crowded it is also a fantastic scene--when the evening lights are lit,flowing with the crowd,having snacks and enjoy the melody of lives.Unleash your passion!

Jasminie（Called moli in Chinese）is a symbol flower of Fuzhou.It is famous for its unique and refreshing scent————not too strong， but to the point，making people be attracted into it.
Jasmine can be used to make tea,within the great scent of itself completely. This kind of tea is a speciality of Fujian Province.But it is widely liked. As a scented tea，Jasmine tea is very popular and drink-tasty. If you would like to chat with me，let me treat you with it！I'm sure that you are to be absorbed in it.

Living near the school,I spent 6 years for my study and lived in Cangshan District,where is a peaceful and beautiful place.
In the leisure time,walking along the street and enjoy the beautiful scene.Or,staying in a cafe to kill a whole afternoon.

This kind of slow life-style might attract anyone who comes here.However，at that time I didn't have engough time to dive into this place filled with secret. Really hoping to come back to here and recall my memories again.
