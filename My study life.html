My study life in Junior and senior high school










Junior High School



Fuzhou Shidai middle school(Shidai means "Times"),the best middle school in Fuzhou.
It used to be a private school of Affiliated High School of Fujian Normal University with beautiful inner scenes.



There are ponds and lots of trees in the school.Various kinds of animals could be spotted here,like frogs and tortoises. We even might come across some squirrels and owls.



The three years of study in Shidai middle school are happy and fulfilling. I learned a lot, made a lot of friends (Keeping in touch with them till now) and finally got into the best local Senior high school with excellent grades. Three fantastic years.It's a pity that this school had been absorbed and became a public school,as a junior section of the Affiliated High School.



Senior High School



Affiliated High School of Fujian Normal University

One of the best senior high schools in Fujian Province. It is a great school with an extremely long history.(More than 140years)

The School motto is so grand:"Take the world as your own responsibility".
The motto had been rooted in my mind forever.My study life here is a continuation of junior high school.

I learn all kinds of knowledge here and actively help teachers and classmates to solve the problems they meet.

What’s more，I have a very good relationship with my teachers. Here, I get a platform to study knowledge, but also a platform to show myself.

Graduated in 2022.It was an unforgettable memory.
